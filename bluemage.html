Blue Mage

Last Updated: 3rd November 2020

Level 60

At level 60, you are allowed up to Rank 5 materia when melding. Both sets use the Incendiario i270 weapon (available after defeating Azulmagia and leveling to 60) with the DPS set melding Crit and the Tank/Healer set melding Spell Speed. None of the gear pieces (except the weapon) overlap for both sets leaving you the option to switch between roles back and forth. (The Mask of Azuro requires you to defeat Siegfried in the Masked Carnavale.)

  • 2.14 GCD: Tank/Healer Mimicry (SpS/DH)
    If you happen to replace all your DH melds for Determination, eat Mushroom Skewer instead of Lemonade.
  • 2.48 GCD: DPS Mimicry (Crit/DH)

Level 70 (Tentative)

The level 70 set will be introduced in Patch 5.4. Assuming that the substats priority remains the same as the level 60 set, we will see the Tank/Healer build using a Spell Speed set once again while the DPS build will be back to Direct Hit and Critical. The highest materia rank allowed to be melded for this level is Rank 6.

  • 2.38 GCD: Tank/Healer Mimicry (SpS/Crit)
    This uses the High Speed BLM Alphascape BiS as a starting template. The weapon (whatever it'll be) will be tentatively melded with two Spell Speed melds. This drops the GCD from 2.39 (shown on Etro) to 2.38. Food of choice will be Kukuru Rusk.
  • 2.48 GCD: DPS Mimicry (Crit/DH)
    This uses the RDM Alphascape BiS as a starting template. The weapon (whatever it'll be) will be tentatively melded with two Direct Hit melds. Food of choice will be Espresso con Panna.
