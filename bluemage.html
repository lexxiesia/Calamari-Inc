Blue Mage

Last Updated: 14th September 2021

Level 60

At level 60, you are allowed up to Rank 5 materia when melding. Both sets use the Incendiario i270 weapon (available after defeating Azulmagia and leveling to 60) with the DPS set melding Crit and the Tank/Healer set melding Spell Speed. None of the gear pieces (except the weapon) overlap for both sets leaving you the option to switch between roles back and forth. (The Mask of Azuro requires you to defeat Siegfried in the Masked Carnavale.)

As of Patch 5.4, syncing down gear should be fine. However, consider grabbing an Incendiario so you still get substats from materia melding as BLU weapons do not give stats whatsoever.

  • 2.14 GCD: Tank/Healer Mimicry (SpS/DH)
    If you happen to replace all your DH melds for Determination, eat Mushroom Skewer instead of Lemonade.
  • 2.48 GCD: DPS Mimicry (Crit/DH)

Level 70

[tl;dr] Get any i400 (or Elemental/Eureka) gear, meld materia, and you'll be fine for all content. If you want to optimize, read below.

Historically, Crit/DH sets are used as a DPS option and SPS/Det for utility on tanks and healers. The current meta seems to be Crit or SPS > Det. In ideal scenarios, Crit/Det has about a 0.5% gain over SpS/Det. However, SPS/Det can feel better to play and is a great pick for all three roles. Pick whatever set suits your playstyle and stick with that.

The one instance where Elemental gear is worse is for O12S if you're going for Revenge Blasts on Ion Efflux. Elemental gear has less VIT so you run the risk of dying depending on your mitigation strategy.

Note: The Predatrice and Friar Rush are interchangeable.

Tank/Healer

  • 2.19 GCD: SpS/Det
    • Omega Belt can be swapped for Yama Belt melded with Determination. This trades main stats for SpS allocation, pushing to the next GCD tier (2.18 GCD).
    • Elemental Pants can be swapped for Omega Pants melded with double SpS materias. This trades extra substats and SpS allocation for INT, dropping down four GCD tiers (2.23 GCD).
    • Ivalician Chest can be swapped for Omega Chest melded with double SpS materias. This trades SpS allocation for extra INT and substats, dropping down four GCD tiers (2.23 GCD).

DPS

  • 2.50 GCD: Big DPS (Crit/Det)
    • Elemental Gloves can be swapped for Mirage Gloves melded with double Crit materias. This swaps your Crit and other substats for extra INT: losing 108 total substats for 10 INT. A budget option only if you really do not have the Elemental Gloves.
    • A.Scaevan Ring can be swapped for Mirage Ring. It's simulated that more stat tiers are achievable with Direct Hit. However, it's better to use double Scaevan if planning on going full Determination. Regardless of the ring used, both are melded with Crit.
  • 2.26 GCD: DoT DPS (SpS/Det)
    • A.Scaevan Gloves can be swapped for Elemental Gloves melded with five SpS materias. If you can be bothered to grind at least one Elemental piece, this is the one piece to take since it's clearly better, even if ever so slightly.
    • Omega Pants can be swapped for Elemental Pants. The second-best piece of Elemental gear to take that is a clear positive. Melded with two SpS and three DH materia, pushing for three extra GCD tiers (2.23 GCD).
    • A.Scaevan Chest can be swapped for Ivalician Chest melded with double DH materias. This trades some main stats and substats for SpS, pushing for three extra GCD tiers (2.23 GCD).
