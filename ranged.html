Patch 5.2 - Patch 5.3: Eden's Verse (i500)

Last Updated: 21st October 2020

Bard

This set follows stat priority except for the melds in the body and weapon, so pay attention to those. You can put the Direct Hit to Determination remelds anywhere you like, but you need exactly 3 of them. There's exactly 1 Determination meld in the bow and 2 in body for compatibility with DNC bis (which wants 2 Determination melds, as opposed to 3).

  • 2.48 GCD: Etro | Ariyala
    • Earring and belt are interchangeable, so if you get a belt from E5S you can buy tome earrings instead.
    • Why the Skill Speed piece? Firstly, Skill Speed gets a small burst of efficiency at low levels because the base speed is just barely under what's needed to hit 2.49 GCD. Secondly, 2.48 GCD allows you to pull off last-second Pitch Perfects in Wanderer's Minuet without clipping.
    • If you play Ninja, you can also swap the Determination melds in the chest with some accessory Direct Hit melds (taking care not to overcap) to share accessories.

For people who clip all the time while double weaving, you can use this set instead. It follows stat priority except for the melds in the body. You can put the Direct Hit to Determination remelds anywhere you like, but you need exactly 2 of them.

Dancer

  • 2.50 GCD: Eden's Verse (Savage)
  • 2.50 GCD: Unreal Trials (i435 Sync)
    For minor gains (0.45% increase in damage), use the Savage set but swap out the weapon and gloves for the i430 versions. Note the melds for stat tiering purposes.

Machinist

  • 2.50 GCD: Etro

Patch 5.0 - Patch 5.1: Eden's Gate (i470) / The Epic of Alexander (Ultimate)

Last Updated: 21st October 2020

Bard

For TEA re-clears, use Ultimate Gandiva. If not, just replace it with Edengrace Harp Bow as shown below.

  • 2.44 GCD: Etro | Ariyala
    This is the standard BiS for most people.
  • 2.50 GCD: Etro | Ariyala
    This is for people with really bad ping and/or clip all the time while double weaving.

Dancer

For TEA re-clears, use Ultimate Twashtar. If not, just replace it with Edengrace Tathlums as shown below.

  • 2.50 GCD: Etro
    If you have Ultimate Twashtar, meld the third slot of the weapon with +60 Determination and change the +60 Determination meld on the Shadowless ring to a +60 Direct Hit meld (the original Determination meld on the ring is for slightly better stat tiering).

Machinist

For TEA re-clears, use Ultimate Armageddon. If not, just replace it with Edengrace Revolver as shown below.

  • 2.50 GCD: Etro
  • 2.44 GCD: Etro
