Patch 6.1: Dragonsong's Reprise (Ultimate)

Last Updated: 21st October 2022

Scholar

A synced Manderville (i615) relic will be equivalent to a crit melded Asphodelos weapon but with 9 extra crit. Feel free to use either the i615 relic or Asphodelos weapon in these sets. The DSR codex remains BiS for DSR once you can get it.

  • High Piety Sets (1008 PIE):
    • Asphodelos Codex
    • Manderville Codex
    • Ultimate Codex of the Heavens
  • Mid Piety Sets (719 PIE):
    • Asphodelos Codex
    • Manderville Codex
    • Ultimate Codex of the Heavens
