Patch 5.2 - Patch 5.3: Eden's Verse (i500)

Last Updated: 20th October 2020

Astrologian

With Patch 5.3, Astrologians went from having the roughest MP economy in 5.x to the probably the best ever in the history of the game (a first for this traditionally mana guzzling job).

i500 Relic (Augmented Solstice and Solstice Recollection are interchangeable) pretty much wins in almost every which way over every other option. However, take note that relic grind might be a hassle to some players.

  • 2.38 GCD: Max Damage Set
    This is the highest damage set that is able to be calculated out following traditional melding methods. Do not be scared of this set, it has a very stable MP economy. It uses the i500 augmented tome weapon.
  • 2.39 GCD: Safer MP Set
    This is the high-damage speedkill set from Patch 5.2. Now this set still has a high amount of damage, is something you likely already have many pieces for, and has a very stable MP economy for pugging.
  • 2.32 GCD: Spell Speed Set
    If you know standard damage theory, then the fact of replacing a bunch of Direct Hit melds for Spell Speed to give more damage isn't a surprise. However, though this may theoretically be viable on paper, initial tests suggest that these are very dangerous and may cause you to run out of MP in the long run.

Scholar

i500 Relic (Augmented Akademos and Akademos Recollection are interchangeable) provides a DPS gain over the Edenchoir and Augmented Crystarium codexes at all relevant Piety levels. However, if you don't have any desire to do the relic grind, you can find the old sets listed here.

  • 2.41 GCD: Recommended
    Higher expected DPS and higher Piety than the old speedkill set.
  • 2.41 GCD: Recommended
    This Piety variant on the weapon is more akin to the old Patch 5.2 recommended set.
  • 2.41 GCD: Speedkill
    WARNING: This set has only 340 Piety! So only use in extremely coordinated environments. Do not use this when pugging and then complain.

White Mage

You can swap between Herring Pie (Crit > SpS food) and Stuffed Highland Cabbage (Det > Crit food) among sets with extremely minimal loss and comfortably dropping a GCD tier.

  • 2.40 GCD (1049 Piety): Recommended
  • 2.40 GCD (838 Piety): Max Damage Set
    i500 Relic (Augmented Ingrimm and Ingrimm Recollection are interchangeable) is best-in-slot for the Max Damage set. Meld Piety in it for comfort or Direct Hit in it for maximum damage.

Patch 5.0 - Patch 5.1: Eden's Gate (i470) / The Epic of Alexander (Ultimate)

Last Updated: 20th October 2020

Astrologian

For TEA re-clears, use Ultimate Deneb. If not, just replace it with Edengrace Planisphere as shown below.

Remember that you don't need too much Piety for TEA, but your BJCC phase will be a lot better if you do consider taking some extra Piety. And as always, take some Super-Ethers.

  • 2.43 GCD: Low Piety
  • 2.38 GCD: High Piety

Scholar

For TEA re-clears, use Ultimate Tetrabiblos. If not, just replace it with Word of Grace as shown below.

  • 2.42/2.41 GCD: Etro
    Very flexible set able to accommodate a wide range of Piety levels and 2 different GCDs. Standard setup has 1889 Piety and a 2.42 GCD and uses Stuffed Highland Cabbage. Swap to Lemon Waffle for 2057 Piety, Herring Pie for 2.41 GCD. You can remove Piety melds for Det/DH as you become more comfortable with the fight (1469 Piety with no melds).

White Mage

For TEA re-clears, use Ultimate Majestas. If not, just replace it with Edengrace Cane as shown below.

Note: Remember to be flexible and PLAY SAFE in Ultimate. Don't avoid Piety because it's Piety! Consider swapping melds and food for more or less Piety as needed.

  • 2.43 GCD (1466 Piety): Etro | Ariyala
  • 2.48 GCD (1268 Piety): Etro | Ariyala
  • 2.43 GCD (1129 Piety): Etro | Ariyala
  • 2.44 GCD (931 Piety): Etro | Ariyala
