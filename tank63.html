Patch 6.3: The Omega Protocol (Ultimate)

Last Updated: 4th April 2023

Unfortunately due to stat tiering, several races (in particular, the 22-strength races: Midlander Hyur, Seeker of the Sun Miqo'te, and Sea Wolf Roegadyn) have slightly different BiS.

Dark Knight

  • Ultimate Omega Claymore:
    • Assuming you are using one of the sets below, the extra slot on the weapon should be melded with DH10.
  • Abyssos Guillotine:
    • 2.50 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
      • Highest theoretical DPS.
      • Uses 2x SkS melds in place of DH.
      • Uses 1x Det meld in place of DH.
    • 2.45 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
      • If you prefer the speed or a particular fight favours the speed.
      • Uses 2x SkS melds in place of DH.
      • Uses 1x Det meld in place of DH.
    • 2.40 GCD: Standard
      • Not recommended, but useable if you prefer the speed.
      • Uses 10x SkS melds in place of DH.
      • Uses 1x SkS9 meld in place of a DH9 on the crafted ring.

Gunbreaker

  • Ultimate Omega Bayonet:
    • 2.50 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
    • 2.45 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
    • 2.42 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
  • Abyssos Sawback:
    • 2.50 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
      • Improved rotational loop.
      • Highest DH rate increases average damage for most encounters.
      • Most lenient choice for high-latency users.
      • Not as lenient for user error.
    • 2.45 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
      • Drifts No Mercy due to poor GCD alignment.
      • Higher DH rate than 2.42.
      • High-latency users might have trouble landing 9 GCDs during No Mercy.
    • 2.42 GCD: [Standard | 22 STR]
      • Very slight drift to No Mercy.
      • Lowest DH of all sets
      • More lenient for high-latency users.

Paladin

  • Ultimate Omega Sword:
    • 2.50 GCD
    • 2.45 GCD
    • 2.42 GCD
  • Abyssos Bastard Sword:
    • 2.50 GCD
    • 2.45 GCD
    • 2.42 GCD

Warrior

  • Ultimate Omega Battleaxe:
    • 2.45 GCD
  • Abyssos War Axe:
    • 2.50 GCD
    • 2.46 GCD
    • 2.45 GCD
    • 2.40 GCD
