Dark Knight

  • 2.50 GCD: Etro
    • You can replace one DH meld with SkS in the 2.50 set to run 2.49 for more wiggle room at a ~0.3% loss, GCD for GCD.
  • 2.45 GCD: Etro
    • The SkS melds to move down from 2.46 to 2.45 are recommended because it is a <0.2% loss, GCD for GCD, in return for better alignment.
    • 2.45 can run Aug. Crafted Ring (Crit + SkS melds) for a 2 DPS loss.
    • 2.45 can also run Aug. Tome Ring (Crit + SkS) with Raid Helm (Crit + Crit) and 1 DH → SkS elsewhere for max Vitality at a ~13 DPS loss.

Gunbreaker

Pick a set that gets you your 9 GCDs under No Mercy. Higher ping might make it difficult to get the 9th GCD of No Mercy reliably on slower speeds. Choose what works for you.

  • 2.45 GCD: Etro
    Feels good on all fights, use if you like 2.45 and don't lose your 9th GCD in No Mercy. Utilizes a crafted ring.
  • 2.43 GCD: Etro
    Works really well when doing Purgation uptime in P1s and feels good in P2/P3/P4s.
  • 2.42 GCD: Etro
  • 2.41 GCD: Etro
    Popular, tried and true, decent on all fights. 2.42 and slower will cost you certain GCDs in Dragonsong's Reprise (Ultimate) so therefore 2.4 or 2.41 is recommended when doing Ultimate.
  • 2.40 GCD: Etro
    The recommended set for Dragonsong.

Paladin

Listed speeds assume food is active. Speeds are primarily chosen for personal preference. Due to differences in latency and execution, certain (typically slower) speeds may be marginally better for certain encounters in optimization (such as 2.45 or 2.44 for Asphodelos).

  • 2.45 GCD: Etro
  • 2.44 GCD: Etro
  • 2.42 GCD: Etro
  • 2.40 GCD: Etro

Warrior

  • 2.50 GCD: Etro
    • No Upheaval drift.
    • No Inner Release drift.
    • The boots melds are Det + Det, giving +3.3 DPS over Crit + Det.
  • 2.46 GCD: Etro
  • 2.45 GCD: Etro
  • 2.40 GCD: Etro
    • Upheaval drifts or has to be clipped every second use.
    • No Inner Release drift.
