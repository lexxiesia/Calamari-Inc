Patch 5.2 - Patch 5.3: Eden's Verse (i500)

Last Updated: 20th October 2020

Dragoon

When choosing one of the below sets, choose it based on what speed is most comfortable for you to play.

  • 2.48 GCD: Etro
    Has a rather comfortable rotation and can't get 9 GCDs in buffs, so less stress.
  • 2.46 GCD: Etro
    Finds a little more leeway with some certain mechanics in Savage.
  • 2.38 GCD: Etro
    This is mainly designed as a faster option for those other-melee mains alt-speccing into Dragoon who find the usual speeds too slow for them to really enjoy it. It is not advised outside of that.

Monk

The slower you get makes a gearset unsurprisingly less suited for those with higher ping, but also unsurprisingly does a little bit more damage. Skill Speed exceeding what you need for the opener and 6 GCD Perfect Balances is almost entirely wasted, so if you’re unsure about it try those on a dummy for a while to see how much speed you can afford to shave off.

  • 1015 Speed Tier: 1025 SkS | 1061 SkS
    It's recommended to go with the 1025 SkS set.
  • 914 Speed Tier: 929 SkS
  • 761 Speed Tier: 785 SkS

Ninja

  • 2.50 GCD: Etro | Ariyala

Samurai

The following three sets are listed in terms of performance from best to worst. Take note that the DPS difference between each set is less than 1%.

  • 2.14 GCD: 28 GCD Rotation
    Requires 2 filler GCDs. Highest performance and most used among all three sets.
  • 2.00 GCD: 30 GCD Rotation
    Requires 4 filler GCDs. Ping unfriendly for double-/iai-weaves.
  • 2.07 GCD: 29 GCD Rotation
    Requires 3 filler GCDs. Easiest buff uptime due to native Gekka/Kasha filler. Also beginner friendly.
