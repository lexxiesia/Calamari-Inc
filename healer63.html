Patch 6.3: The Omega Protocol (Ultimate)

Last Updated: 4th April 2023

For jobs not listed below, please use one of the Tome Ring sets from your job above as vitality is important when progging an Ultimate fight. And as usual, bring plenty of ethers.

Sage

NOTE: These sets have Det melds, but you can easily meld DH as well. DH is ahead in damage by a minimal amount, but a Det melded set provides ~2-3% more healing. Choose whatever sounds better to you.

  • Abyssos Wings:
    • 2.50 GCD (877 PIE)
    • 2.47 GCD (741 PIE)

Scholar

  • 2.40 GCD: A fairly nice GCD for getting some extra slidecasts during Pantokrator while giving pretty good alignment.
    • 741 PIE | This set has a very safe amount of piety for this fight as it doesn't drain too much MP.
    • 526 PIE | Because Omega has such low MP pressure, it's pretty safe to run with PIE this low for prog and moreso for reclears.

White Mage

NOTE: This set is identical to the SCH set and works well on every healer. Feel free to swap DH melds to SpS if you prefer faster sets or Det if you prefer more raw healing, neither of them will make a huge difference in damage.

  • 2.40 GCD:
    • Abyssos Staff (526 PIE)

Patch 6.1: Dragonsong's Reprise (Ultimate)

Last Updated: 4th April 2023

Astrologian

  • 2.32 GCD:
    • Asphodelos Orrery (479 PIE)
    • Ultimate Heavensmeter (390 PIE)
    • Amazing Manderville Torquetum (479 PIE)

Sage

NOTE: If you do not yet have the Ultimate Milpreves, the Manderville Milpreves is slightly better than the Asphodelos Pendulums.

  • 2.45 GCD:
    • Ultimate Milpreves of the Heavens (479 PIE)
    • Manderville Milpreves (593 PIE)

Scholar

The piety requirements vary depending on your Phase 7 strat for Akh Morns. 3-3-2 requires more GCD healing while 6-1-1 is a little more lax.

NOTE: A synced i615 Manderville Relic will be equivalent to a Crit melded Asphodelos Codex but with +9 extra Crit. Feel free to use either the relic or codex in these sets. Ultimate Codex of the Heavens remains BiS for DSR.

  • 2.45 GCD:
    • 701 PIE | A very good amount of piety for prog and a slow GCD to help conserve MP. Suggested if you're currently progging the fight.
    • 479 PIE | If you use this set with 3-3-2 or you're unsure how to properly manage your mana, you will run out. Suggested if you want to optimize after clearing.

White Mage

These sets have sufficient piety for prog without sacrificing too much damage, but feel free to adjust them based on your groups personal needs and always bring tons of HQ Super-Ethers. The Det melds in these sets can all be swapped for DH melds if you want a bit more damage and don't need the extra healing.

NOTE: A synced i615 Manderville Relic is slightly better than the Asphodelos Cane (by 9 Crit). Feel free to use either the relic or cane in these sets. Ultimate Cane of the Heavens remains BiS for DSR.

  • 2.45 GCD:
    • 701 PIE | This is a higher piety set aimed for people progging for the first time especially if you are running the 3-3-2 Phase 7 strat which requires substantially more GCD healing. This set also gives more wiggle room for raises. You can add or remove piety melds as you progress, or look to one of the higher damage sets.
    • 479 PIE | This is a higher damage set mostly aimed at people running the 6-1-1 Phase 7 strategy or for reclears. This set should be avoided if you are progging and using a 3-2-2 P7 strat.
