Patch 5.2 - Patch 5.3: Eden's Verse (i500)

Last Updated: 19th October 2020

Dark Knight

Before picking your BiS, make sure you're able to hit 5 GCDs in Blood Weapon constantly at that GCD.

2.43 is theoretically the highest DPS only if you can get 5 GCDs in Blood Weapon. You want to go as slow as possible while still hitting 5 GCDs in Blood Weapon. Faster GCDs can allow for better GCD timing during downtime or when the boss jumps.

Prioritize whatever GCD is comfortable for you as the damage difference between sets is negligible.
* Meld Determination instead of Direct Hit if you play Warrior.

  • 2.43 GCD: No Relic | Relic Helm & Boots
  • 2.41 GCD: No Relic | Relic Helm
  • 2.40 GCD: Relic Helm
  • 2.38 GCD: No Relic | Relic Helm

Gunbreaker

  • 2.42 GCD - Sausage and Sauerkraut (i500): Etro
    Works well in every fight. Less Direct Hit than 2.40 but more STR. The three sets are negligible in damage variance.
  • 2.40 GCD - Sausage and Sauerkraut (i499): Etro
    Uses Idealized i480 headpiece to build heavy Direct Hit. Works well in every fight. Very good for a 2.40 preference. Added Direct Hit helps offset loss in STR.
  • 2.42 GCD - Stuffed Highland Cabbage (i499): Etro
    Another very competitive option.

Paladin

2.42 and 2.40 are the recommended speeds for most players. Slower sets are stronger in a vacuum due to Direct Hit melds, but faster sets may line up better with encounter mechanics and/or compensate for high latency during Fight or Flight. Use whichever is personally comfortable and make rotation adjustments for alignment where needed.

  • 2.42 GCD - Sausage and Sauerkraut: Etro
  • 2.40 GCD - Sausage and Sauerkraut: Etro
  • 2.39 GCD - Sausage and Sauerkraut: Etro

Warrior

  • 2.43 GCD: Etro
    With Patch 5.3's Storm's Eye changes, we can go a bit slower. This set is not comfy but offers higher theoretical DPS and requires well-planned rotations in fights. All the potions and buffs must be used late into the GCD to ensure maximum effects.
  • 2.38 GCD: Ariyala | Etro
    Note that using the Tome helm is ~0.03 dps better on average. Get either of the helms and meld accordingly.
  • 2.37 GCD: Ariyala | Etro
  • 2.36 GCD: Ariyala | Etro

Patch 5.0 - Patch 5.1: Eden's Gate (i470) / The Epic of Alexander (Ultimate)

Last Updated: 12th February 2021

Dark Knight

For TEA re-clears with Ultimate Nothung, use the following sets . If not, just replace it with Edengrace Greatsword.

  • 2.40 GCD: Etro
  • 2.38 GCD: Etro

Gunbreaker

For TEA re-clears with Ultimate Anarchy, use the following set. If not, just replace it with Edengrace Manatrigger.

  • 2.42 GCD: Etro
    The leg piece on this set do not need materia since item level syncing nullifies materia slots on the individual pieces that are over the item level sync threshold.
  • 2.40 GCD: Etro
    The final step of the i515 Relic in Patch 5.45 allows for adding of substats. Max Crit and Det, then pump into Skill Speed for your Relic! Additionally, the new relic can be used to max substats in UCoB and in UWU. Guaranteed all 4 stats cap in UCoB, 3/4 in UWU (unless otherwise mistaken).

Paladin

For TEA re-clears with Ultimate Almace, use the following sets. If not, just replace it with Edengrace Bastard Sword.

Note: Melds on synced gear are ignored, but built-in stats will be capped if the gear's original item level is high enough. This is why Paladins always use the highest iLv shields that are available to them whenever they're attempting Ultimate content.

  • 2.42 GCD: Etro
  • 2.40 GCD: Etro

i515 Relic sword and shield are a significant improvement for synced content, if available.
